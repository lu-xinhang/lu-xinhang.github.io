CV

I am a postdoc in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of New South Wales, working in the Algorithmic Decision Theory (ADT) Group led by Haris Aziz and Toby Walsh and the Algorithms Group led by Serge Gaspers. Prior to this, I spent some wonderful time working as a postdoc with Warut Suksompong at the National University of Singapore and completed my PhD at Nanyang Technological University, where I was very fortunate to be advised by Xiaohui Bei.

I am broadly interested in problems at the interface between computer science and economics. Recently, my work has focused on mechanism design and fairness in algorithmic decision-making (e.g., resource allocation, collective choice).
